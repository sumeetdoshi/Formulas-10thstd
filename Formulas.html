Formulas For Cuboid

  1. Surface area of Cuboid.
    L=Length
    B=Breath
    H=Height.=2(LB+BH+LH)
  2. Lateral Surface area of cuboid = 2(L + B)H
  3. Diagonal of Cuboid=√L2+ B2+ H 2
  4. Volume of cuboid = LBH
  5. Length of all 12 edges of cuboids = 4(L+B+H)

Formulas For Cube

  1. Surface area of Cube of side L = 6L2
  2. Lateral surface area of cube = 4L2
  3. Diagonal of cube = L√3
  4. Volume of cube = L3

Formulas For Right Circular Cylinder

  1. Lateral or Curved Surface area of cylinder = 2*π*radius*Height
  2. Total Surface area of cylinder = 2*π*radius(height+radius)
  3. Volume of Cylinder = π*radius*radius*height

Formulas For Right Circular Cone

  1. Curved Surface area of cone = π*radius*L
  2. Total surface area of cone = π*radius(L+radius)
  3. Volume of cone = 1/3*π*radius*radius*H

Formulas For Sphere

  1. Surface area of Sphere = 4πr2
  2. Volume of sphere = 4/3πr3
  3. Curved surface area of hemisphere = 2πr2
  4. Volume of hemisphere = 2/3πr3
  5. Volume of spherical shell whose outer and inner radii and R and r respectively = 4/3π(R2-r2)
